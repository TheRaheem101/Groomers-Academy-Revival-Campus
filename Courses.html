Our Courses

English Grammer And Language Course

The Groomers Academy Revival Campus Conducts A Grammer Course Every Year Which Lasts For About 2 And A Half Month. In This Course Students Are Taught About The English Language And Also Taught About The Grammer Part.

Benifits Of This Course:

  1. → The Students Will Be Able To Understand English Subject And Language Easily.


  2. → The Students Will Be Able To Speak In English With Others.


  3. → The Vocabulary Of The Students Will Increase And They Will Imrove Their English Speaking.


  4. → The Students Will Able To Understand The Grammer Of English.


Computer Course

The Groomers Academy Revival Campus Conducts A Computer Course Every Year Which Lasts For About 2 And A Half Month. In This Course The Students Are Taught About MS Excel, MS Word, MS Powerpoint, And Many Other Softwares.

Benifits Of This Course:

  1. → The Students Will Be Able To Use Basic Office Tools.


  2. → The Students Will Be Able To Make Documentations And Persentations.


  3. → The Students Will Be Able To Enter Data In MS Excel.


  4. → The Students Will Be Able To Use Computer/Laptop More Faster.
